The mysterious protagonists in my paintings are often shown in a dialogue with their counterparts creating a dramatic symphony of real and unreal. After graduating from M.S University, Baroda, I have exhibited my work in severalone-man shows and over 35 group shows across India. I mostly use oil on canvas as my media to bring out the vibrancy in my paintings.
